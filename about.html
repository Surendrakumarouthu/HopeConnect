About Us

HopeConnect is a dedicated platform aimed at connecting vulnerable groups, including orphans, the elderly, and the handicapped, with nearby support services. Founded by Routhu Surendra Kumar, a passionate MEAN Stack Developer, our mission is to create a compassionate community where everyone has access to the support they need.

Our Vision

At Hope Connect, our vision is to create a supportive and compassionate network that bridges the gap between those in need and the resources available to help them. We aim to empower individuals and organizations to contribute to social welfare, making support accessible for vulnerable communities such as orphans, the elderly, and people with disabilities. Together, we aspire to foster a world where kindness and community drive meaningful change.
